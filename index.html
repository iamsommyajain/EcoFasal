EcoFasal is a Sustainable Agriculture Advisory Platform that harnesses the power of AI to revolutionize farming. With the Personalized Crop Recommendation using AI, farmers receive advice on the most profitable and sustainable crops based on local soil data, climate patterns, and market trends. Additionally, our AI-Driven Pest and Disease Detection system utilizes advanced technologies to identify threats early, allowing for timely intervention and targeted treatments. The platform provides Sustainable Farming Tutorials, equipping farmers with essential knowledge and skills to adopt eco-friendly practices. Together, these features promote resilience, productivity, and sustainability in modern agriculture.
